Algorithm Lab

An overview of the advanced Data Structures and Algorithms powering AlgoMart.

QuickSort

Use: Dynamic product sorting by price and rating.

Complexity: O(n log n) average. Uses divide-and-conquer strategy.

Binary Search

Use: Lightning-fast product lookup by name.

Complexity: O(log n). Requires pre-sorted list.

0/1 Knapsack

Use: Budget optimization. Picks items to maximize rating within a cost limit.

Complexity: O(n × Budget). Uses Dynamic Programming.

Prim's Algorithm

Use: Greedy discount path. Minimizes cost by connecting category bundles.

Complexity: O(E log V). Finds Minimum Spanning Tree of discounts.
